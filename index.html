Welcome

This database allows users to search for free UX tools for conducting research and producing documents. Users can specify the kind of tool they want using the checklist on the left and then search to receive a list of relevant tools and their features. This database is assembled by members of the eValuation and User Experience (VUE) Lab at the University of Alabama in Huntsville.

The VUE lab is a dedicated space for user experience (UX) research and thus can be used for learning, collaboration, design, and evaluation. It is open to UAH faculty/students and to community members. More information on the VUE Lab can be found here: https://www.uah.edu/ahs/departments/english/vuelab
