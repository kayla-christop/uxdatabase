Welcome

This database allows users to search for free UX tools for conducting research and producing documents. Users can determine the kind of tool they want by using the checklist on the left and then filter to receive a list of tools relevant to the specified categories and any optional features that best fit what they are looking for. If users wish to find a plathora of options that are applicable, but not specific to, certain functions, they also have the option to filter for tools that may not be the best option for the intended task, but serve to offer a wider variety of tools to choose from.

This database is assembled by members of the eValuation and User Experience (VUE) Lab at the University of Alabama in Huntsville.The VUE lab is a dedicated space for user experience (UX) research and thus can be used for learning, collaboration, design, and evaluation. It is open to UAH faculty/students and to community members. More information on the VUE Lab can be found here: https://www.uah.edu/ahs/departments/english/vuelab
